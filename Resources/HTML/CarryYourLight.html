Carry Your Light

The multitalented singer-songwriter, C'est Lis, hired me once again to design her cover art and promo graphics for her holiday song Carry Your Light.  We used photos taken by Bear Hand Photography and used Lis's handwriting to create the overall aesthetic.

C'est Lis Website

The Process (2019)

Single Cover

With Christmas in mind, we decided to use a poinsettia to subtly suggest the holiday spirit. I also used a white textured border that looks similar to wrapping paper. The edges of the photograph are cut to off to as if the photograph has been inserted into the layer behind it.

As you can see there is a bright yellow light glowing behind Lis. A friend of hers, after hearing her song, said she pictured, "this girl walking down the street in New York on a snowy winter's day. Everything was black and white except for the color around her. As she walked, she added color to those around her and even to the store fronts. Basically, she was bringing life and light to those around her."
