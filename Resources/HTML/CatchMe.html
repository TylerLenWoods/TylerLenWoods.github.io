Catch Me If You Can

The very talented singer-songwriter, C'est Lis, hired me to design her cover art and promo graphics. What I really enjoyed about this project was the quality of my canvas. The photography by Nathan Petersen was professional and elegant. It was a joy to be part of such a creative team.

C'est Lis Website

The Process (2019)

Single Cover

Simple, elegant, and subtle, the title design merely steps to the side in order to showcase the beautiful photo of C'est Lis. Never forget that sometimes less is, in fact, more.

As colors go, I wanted it to feel natural. I did not saturate the image at all actually. I didn't have to. I only adjusted the brightness and contrast which is another reason I appreciated Nathan's camera work. The lighting, the depth, and the clarity all came from a well-thought-out photographer. He made my job much easier.

Social Media Promos

Using Nathan Petersen's raw image photos, I never had an issue with losing quality as I cropped in Photoshop. After C'est Lis was able to find a font she liked, I was then able to arrange the words of her lyrics around the images. I hope that as you see the words and images, you can see how the design helps to convey the message.

C'est Lis was able to use these lyric promos on Facebook and Instagram in order to gain marketing momentum before the song release. This campaign lasted over a month.

Designs We Didn't Use

Unfortunately, in the design world, or in any creative world for that matter, you cannot use everything. These were the designs we had to discard after we went in a new direction for the visual story. It was the right decision for sure. However, I'm very proud of these as well so I wanted to put them up for you to see.
