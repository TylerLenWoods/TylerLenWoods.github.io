SoFar Website

The Process (2019)

SoFar Poster

As you can see, the design is primarily text driven. The grid layout neatly organizes and prioritizes the information with varying levels of importance. Within the header, the SoFar Sounds logo sits right above the venue "OnTheOne". The date and time are conveniently placed inside the header at the right hand side of the poster.

Each of the three artists are hightlighted boldly and clearly, all with equal importance. The right side of the page utilizes Sofar's branding with "Eclectic Artists" and "Intimate Setting". There is also two social media tags for both SoFar Sounds and OnTheOne.

Further down the right side of the page provides the exact address of the venue, a reminder that the event is live, and a BYOB detail that many would find helpful.

Using Nathan Petersen's raw image photos, I never had an issue with losing quality as I cropped in Photoshop. After C'est Lis was able to find a font she liked, I was then able to arrange the words of her lyrics around the images. I hope that as you see the words and images, you can see how the design helps to convey the message.
