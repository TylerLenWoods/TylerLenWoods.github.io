About

I'm a freelance Illustrator and Graphic Designer who loves to travel and do creative things. Currently, I live in Greensboro, NC with my beautiful wife, Naomi. I have been freelancing since 2018, working with a variety of clients ranging from singer-songwriters, authors, game developers, and even podcasts. While I am self-taught, I am very excited to start an associates degree in Graphic Design starting in 2020 from Penn Foster College.

Services

So you may be wondering. What do I do? I simply help others tell their story better using visual media and communication. Do you need album artwork? I can do that. Do you need your website to look great? I can do that. Need a logo? Book cover? Social Media promos? I've got you. Maybe your a software engineer who needs graphics for games. I can help there too. To sum it all up, I offer traditional & digital illustration, web & graphic design, and 2D game design. Interested? Contact me for prices.
